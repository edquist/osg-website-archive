OSG Research Highlights

We publish a monthly Research Highlights article where you can read about exciting research being done using the OSG. We also send out sporadic updates about various events, interesting news items or other pointers.

If you would like to subscribe to receive these updates, please click [email protected] to open a new email and then follow the instructions below:

  1. Leave the subject line blank.
  2. Type SUBSCRIBE OSG YOURFIRSTNAME YOURLASTNAME in the body of the message.
    So, if your name is John Smith, type SUBSCRIBE OSG JOHN SMITH
  3. There should be no other text at all in the email.

If you would like to unsubscribe from these updates, please click [email protected] to open a new email and the follow then instructions below:

  1. Leave the subject line blank
  2. Type UNSUBSCRIBE OSG in the body of the message (no need for your name this time).
    So, if your name is John Smith, type UNSUBSCRIBE OSG
  3. There should be no other text at all in the email.
