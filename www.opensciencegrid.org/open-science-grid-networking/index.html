Open Science Grid Networking

The Open Science Grid (OSG) is uniquely positioned to support its members and affiliates in all areas of networking.  OSG runs a number of networking services designed to gather needed network metrics, identify network problems and understand network capabilities. OSG members are encouraged to contact the OSG networking team with questions.  For example:

  • How is my network performing?
  • Is my network causing problems for my site?
  • What can I do to improve my networking capability?
  • I think there is something wrong with the network; what do I do?

To better support end-users, sites and virtual organizations (VOs), OSG strongly recommends the deployment of perfSONAR network measurement points (toolkits).

If network problems are suspected, please open a support ticket with the OSG GOC.
