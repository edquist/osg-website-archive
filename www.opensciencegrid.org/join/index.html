Please select from the following options.

I am a U.S. researcher, and I would like to use the Open Science Grid.

If you are a U.S. campus researcher and you would like to use distributed high-throughput computing to further your project, please see OSG Connect to get started. All you need to sign up is an academic or research need for additional computing cycles and a network identity issued by your home academic institution.

I represent a U.S. research group that would like to use / share computing resources with OSG.

If you want to form a VO, want to connect your computing resources to the OSG production fabric, or just use the OSG “opportunistic” resources, we can provide technical recommendations and support on how best to benefit from the OSG; we will work with you to understand your needs and help develop a plan that enables you to do science on the OSG. Please email us at [email protected] to get started.

I am not sure how best to use OSG and want to consult with an OSG staff member.

If you would like to use OSG, but you are not sure where to start, please contact OSG user support through [email protected]. We will set up a phone call with you to discuss your needs and recommend the appropriate path for you.
